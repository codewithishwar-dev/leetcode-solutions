# Notes - 13. Roman to Integer

## 🧠 Patterns

* Left < Right → subtract
* Else → add

## ⚠️ Tricky Cases

* IV → 4
* IX → 9
* XL → 40
* XC → 90
* CD → 400
* CM → 900

## 💡 Optimization Insight

* Right-to-left traversal removes need for lookahead
